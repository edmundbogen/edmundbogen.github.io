What “Off-Market” Really Means in Boca Raton Luxury Real Estate

It doesn’t mean secret. It doesn’t mean rule-free. Done properly, it means one thing: a documented process the seller directs — in writing.

Buyers picture a hidden marketplace only certain agents can unlock. Sellers imagine a quiet handshake sale with no paperwork. Both pictures are wrong — and with private-listing practices under a national microscope in 2026, the gap between the myth and the rules has never mattered more.

This week: the four marketing paths every seller can choose from — full MLS, Coming Soon, delayed marketing, and office exclusive — what privacy honestly costs, the one-business-day rule that governs public marketing, and the five questions to ask any agent who pitches you an off-market sale.
