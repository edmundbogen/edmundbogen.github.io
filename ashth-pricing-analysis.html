Never spends more than 15% of gross revenue on marketing. Profitable on every first order. Primary acquisition: Instagram + affiliates. The lean DTC model.

VRAI (Diamond Foundry)

VC-Backed / Luxury

Pandora

Global / Mass Market

Key insight: No small-to-mid DTC lab-grown diamond brand has publicly documented a successful EU market entry from scratch. Brands that succeeded had $100M+ backing or were established global players.

How to structure the deal
1
Paid Discovery

€3K - 5K (credited against engagement)

Market audit, competitive landscape, recommended strategy, draft SOW. Filters serious buyers and funds proposal development.

2
Months 1-3: Project-Based

Strategy + Build Phase

Market entry strategy, website internationalization, Amazon store setup. Higher one-time fees, defined deliverables and milestones.

3
Months 4-12: Retainer

Execution + Optimization

Paid media, SEO, influencer campaigns, reporting. Monthly retainer with quarterly business reviews.

+
Performance Layer

Base retainer + 10% of revenue above baseline

Quarterly true-ups with multi-touch attribution. Aligns incentives and demonstrates confidence in delivery. 30-day attribution window (extended for luxury purchase cycles).

Risk Assessment
Critical risk factors
Top risks for ConsciousCarats EU expansion, based on documented DTC failure patterns.
High

Undercapitalization

45% of DTC failures are from undercapitalization. €25K/mo may not sustain 5-market expansion. Phased approach is essential.

High

Lab-Grown Diamond Price Erosion

Prices dropping to near-marginal production cost. Only 23% of consumers attribute emotional value to lab-grown (vs. 89% natural). Brand storytelling is critical.

High

Localization Failure

What works in India won't necessarily translate to UK/EU. Each market requires localized messaging, not just translation.

Med

EU Regulatory Complexity

GDPR, hallmarking, VAT/IOSS, consumer protection differ by country. 44% of e-commerce leaders cite compliance as top concern.

Med

Brexit Logistics

UK fulfillment cannot easily serve EU (25% tariff impact). Need separate UK + EU logistics infrastructure.

Med

Competitive Saturation

Pandora, VRAI, and local players already in market. Differentiation through sustainability/ethics is the entry point.

Strategic Recommendations
How we should price this
1

Anchor on Tier 2, Start with Tier 1

Tier 2 (€423K/yr) is the sweet spot between capability and value. If client's €25K/mo includes ad spend, Tier 1 is the realistic starting point.

2

Charge for Discovery

€3-5K paid discovery phase, credited against engagement. Qualifies serious buyers, funds proposal development, demonstrates value before commitment.

3

Phase the Engagement

Months 1-3: Project-based strategy + build. Months 4-12: Monthly retainer for execution. De-risks client commitment and proves value first.

4

Use Hybrid Pricing

€15K/mo base + 8% of EU revenue above €50K/mo. Aligns incentives and demonstrates confidence in delivery.

5

UK-First is the Smart Play

English-language, largest luxury market, strongest Amazon marketplace. Position phased approach as strategic, not a limitation.

6

Leverage Portugal

Eytan's Portugal presence = low-cost EU execution. Position it as test market before scaling to UK/DE/FR. Competitive advantage others can't match.

7

Target 45%+ Gross Margin

Use offshore/freelance for multilingual content. Keep strategy, client management, and media buying in-house. Pass through ad spend transparently.
