The Luxury Capital Moved South: Florida Overtakes New York in Million-Dollar Listings

For the first time in nearly a decade, New York is no longer America’s capital of million-dollar listings — South Florida is. And the metro that took the crown runs straight through Palm Beach.

Realtor.com’s 2026 luxury outlook puts the Miami–Fort Lauderdale–West Palm Beach metro at 10,513 active million-dollar listings at year-end 2025 — decisively ahead of New York’s 9,216.
