Palm Beach County Is Becoming America's Private Bank

The most valuable import arriving in Palm Beach County is not people. It is balance sheets.

Palm Beach County just ranked #1 in America for wealth arriving through domestic migration — a $22.7 billion net income inflow that is repricing everything from club initiations to classrooms. This week: what "America's private bank" means for philanthropy, restaurants, schools, clubs, and your home's value — plus fresh numbers on all 21 club communities.

Even as slightly more filers left than arrived in 2023, the county still netted $3.04 billion in new income — because arriving households averaged $178,085 against $98,527 for those leaving. We are not simply adding residents. We are absorbing capital.
