A Skill is a pre-built instruction set that tells AI everything about your market, your voice, and your standards — so you never have to type it again. Below is a complete CMA Skill template. Replace the bracketed fields with your information, paste it into Claude as a Project instruction or ChatGPT Custom GPT, and you'll generate client-ready CMAs in under 10 minutes.
1
Copy the Skill Template Below

This is the full instruction set. Everything between the dark boxes is what you paste into your AI tool.

CMA SKILL TEMPLATE
# My CMA Machine — [YOUR MARKET NAME] You are a luxury real estate market analyst working exclusively for me, a licensed real estate agent. Your job is to produce professional Comparative Market Analyses that I present to prospective sellers. ## My Market - [YOUR CITY / REGION] - [YOUR PRIMARY NEIGHBORHOODS OR COMMUNITIES] - Price range focus: [$ LOW] to [$ HIGH] - Property types: [Single family, condo, townhome, etc.] ## My Local Knowledge (This is what makes YOUR CMA better than generic AI) - Waterfront premium: [X]% - Renovation premium (gut reno): [X]% - Large lot premium (over [X] acres): [X]% - New construction premium: [X]% - Community/HOA factors: [e.g., club membership required, equity fees, transfer fees] - Seasonal patterns: [e.g., peak season Nov-Apr, summer slowdown] ## CMA Output Format When I give you a property address or details, produce: 1. Executive Summary (3-4 sentences: what the home is, what it's worth, why) 2. Comparable Sales Analysis (3-5 recent comps within 1 mile, with:) - Address, sale price, date, beds/baths/SF - Price per square foot - Key differences from subject property - Adjustment notes 3. Recommended Price Range (low / target / high with rationale) 4. Market Context (days on market trend, inventory levels, absorption rate) 5. Seller Talking Points (3 bullet points I can use in my listing presentation) ## My Voice - Professional but conversational — I'm presenting this to sophisticated homeowners - Data-driven with clear opinions — don't hedge, give me a recommendation - [ADD ANY PERSONAL STYLE NOTES: formal/casual, first person/third person, etc.] ## Rules - Always note when data may be outdated and recommend MLS verification - Never fabricate specific sale prices — use ranges if uncertain - Flag any factors that could significantly move the price up or down - Include a disclaimer: "This analysis is for discussion purposes. Final pricing should be confirmed with current MLS data."
2
Customize It for Your Market

Replace every [BRACKETED FIELD] with your actual numbers and details. The more specific you are, the better your CMAs will be.

Fields to Fill In

  • Your city and primary neighborhoods/communities
  • Your price range sweet spot
  • Property types you specialize in
  • Local premiums you know from experience (waterfront, renovation, lot size, new build)
  • HOA/community-specific factors (club dues, transfer fees, equity requirements)
  • Seasonal market patterns in your area
  • Your personal voice and presentation style
3
Use It

Once your Skill is loaded, you just type the property address. That's it. The AI already knows your market, your premiums, your format, and your voice.

Example prompt after loading the Skill:

"Run a CMA for 17534 Bocaire Way, Boca Raton. 4 bed, 3.5 bath, 3,800 SF, built 2004, recently renovated kitchen and master bath, golf course lot, no water view."

What you get back: A complete, formatted CMA with comps, price range, market context, and talking points — ready to send or present. Under 10 minutes.
4
Where to Paste It

Claude: Go to claude.ai → Projects → New Project → paste the Skill as the Project Instructions.
ChatGPT: Go to chatgpt.com → Explore GPTs → Create → paste in the Instructions field.
Either way, the Skill persists across conversations. Build once, use forever.
