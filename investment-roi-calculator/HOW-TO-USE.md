# Investment ROI Calculator — How to Use

**Link:** https://edmundbogen.github.io/investment-roi-calculator/

This tool tells us how close an investment property gets to our **6% cash-on-cash return** target. Your job: find candidate properties and enter accurate numbers.

## Steps

1. **Open the link.** It loads with South Florida defaults already filled in.
2. **Property** — type the address/community and the **purchase price**.
3. **Rental Income** — enter a **representative monthly rent** for that *exact* area. Use comparable rentals in the same neighborhood — this is the most important number you research.
4. **Operating Expenses** — defaults are pre-filled (taxes, insurance, capex, management, etc.). Edit any line if you have a real figure for that specific property (HOA/club dues and CDD especially).
5. **Purchase & Financing** — choose **All Cash** or **Financed**. Financed reveals down payment, rate, and term.
6. **Read the results panel** (right side):
   - The big **cash-on-cash %** with a green / amber / red verdict vs. the 6% goal.
   - **"To hit your target"** tells you the rent OR the max price needed to actually reach 6%. This is the key output.
7. Click **"View Buyer Summary"** for a clean, client-ready one-page version.

## ⚠️ Important: nothing is saved centrally

**This tool does NOT save your work to any database or send it to anyone.** If you close the tab without doing one of the steps below, your numbers are gone.

### How to keep / send your work — two ways:

- **Copy Shareable Link** (button on the results panel): copies a link that has all your entered numbers baked in. Paste it into an email/text to Edmund and it opens pre-loaded with exactly what you entered. **This is how you send a finished analysis.**
- **Print / PDF** (button on the results panel, or your browser's Print): switches to the Buyer Summary and saves a clean PDF. Use "Save as PDF" as the printer.

> Tip: your own browser remembers the last analysis you ran, but only on that device. To share or archive it, use **Copy Shareable Link** or **Print / PDF**.
