Timeline & Deadlines

A relocation runs on dates. Here's the sequence that keeps your move on track — check items off as you go.

8 Weeks Out
Define your search & budget
Set neighborhoods, rent-vs-buy decision, and a target move date with your concierge.
6 Weeks Out
Tour homes & verify schools
Schedule property tours; confirm school boundaries for your top addresses.
4 Weeks Out
Secure your home
Sign lease or go under contract. Request moving-company quotes (see Module 4).
3 Weeks Out
Book your mover
Confirm the moving date with your interstate carrier; reserve elevators / certificates of insurance if needed.
2 Weeks Out
Set up services
Transfer/establish utilities, internet, mail forwarding, and update your address.
Move Week
Arrive & enroll
Walk-through, key handoff, and submit your school-enrollment packet.
Settling In
Florida essentials
Driver's license, vehicle registration, voter registration, and homestead exemption (if buying).

Master Move Checklist

  • ✓Neighborhood & budget confirmed
  • ✓Home secured (lease signed / under contract)
  • ✓School boundaries verified
  • ✓Moving company booked
  • ✓Utilities & internet scheduled
  • ✓Mail forwarding & address changes
  • ✓School enrollment packet submitted
  • ✓Florida driver's license & registration
