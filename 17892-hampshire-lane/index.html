The Community

Life at Stonebridge Country Club

Set on roughly 400 acres off Clint Moore Road in West Boca Raton, Stonebridge Country Club is an established, guard-gated community of just under 400 homes built around an 18-hole championship golf course — originally designed by Karl Litten and fully reimagined in a Kipp Schulties redesign.

At its center is a recently modernized clubhouse with multiple dining venues, a full-service fitness center and spa, Har-Tru tennis, pickleball, and a resort pool — all wrapped in the gracious, relaxed Palm Beach lifestyle the community is known for.

Membership in Stonebridge Country Club is mandatory for homeowners; a one-time membership contribution of $150,000 applies per the listing. Club dues, fees, and membership terms are set by the association and should be verified directly with Stonebridge Country Club.
