Strategic Recommendation: Separate the Brands

David currently has CVL nested inside his consulting website. This is wrong. Eytan's strong recommendation: separate the community component from the consulting component. Two linked but distinct properties.

Same lesson we learned with REIGNation vs. Bogen.ai — the community is not the service provider. Users get confused. SEO suffers. Separate them, link them, let each breathe.
