Executive summary

The thesis

The original plan positions AI as both the biggest business opportunity and the biggest trap. The winning posture is not full automation or full skepticism, but knowing how to exploit the 80% AI does well while monetizing the human 20% that still drives trust, negotiation, and execution.

Bogen’s Law

AI handles lead generation workflows, listing descriptions, market analysis, nurture sequences, content production, and administrative automation. The Bogen Method teaches that layer, but the premium value sits in implementation quality and judgment.

The final 20%

  • Judgment under uncertainty
  • Relationship leverage
  • Negotiation and closing
  • Strategic positioning
  • Implementation oversight
  • Human trust and authority
Offer architecture

The four-tier ladder

Each tier maps to a different level of implementation intensity, client maturity, and price sensitivity, moving from learning the 80% to full implementation and enterprise advisory.

Tier 1

Mastermind Membership — $497/mo

Weekly live mastermind, private community, monthly AI tool teardown, template library, and a first-win milestone of one AI-built asset in the first week.

Tier 2

Coaching+ Cohort — $2,500/mo

Weekly cohort with Edmund, Loom reviews, private channel support, one automation build per month from Eytan’s team, and monthly 1-on-1 coaching.

Tier 3

Done-With-You AI — $10,000/mo

12-month implementation partnership with weekly strategy calls, dedicated backend build time, quarterly reviews, priority access, and measurable revenue impact targets.

Tier 4

Enterprise / Advisory — $50,000+

Phase 3 only. Invite-only AI transformation work for brokerages, multi-location operators, and enterprise teams, built on proof gathered from Tiers 1–3.
