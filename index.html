About Edmund

Edmund Bogen is a multifaceted entrepreneur and luxury real estate specialist operating three complementary ventures in South Florida. As leader of The Edmund Bogen Team at Douglas Elliman Real Estate, he specializes in the luxury market with particular expertise in Saint Andrews Country Club and prestigious gated communities throughout South Florida.


With an average transaction value of $1.9M and annual production of $39.9M across 21 transactions, Edmund maintains market dominance through sophisticated market knowledge combined with deep community integration. His approach focuses on relationship-building rather than transactional sales.


Beyond real estate, Edmund has established himself as a technology-forward business coach and platform developer, helping real estate professionals leverage AI tools while maintaining authentic client relationships. He leads a thriving group coaching program and provides individual strategic consulting to agents nationwide.
