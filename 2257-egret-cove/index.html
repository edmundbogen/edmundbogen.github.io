The Community

Welcome to Baywinds

Built by Lennar and bordered by serene wetlands, Baywinds is a 1,082-home gated 55+ community less than 10 miles from downtown West Palm Beach. The 7,000-square-foot Club Baywinds is the heartbeat — full-service fitness, resort pool, lap pool, spa, tennis, bocce, and a year-round social calendar run by a full-time activities director.

It's the rare community where you can be as active — or as private — as you want to be. World-class beaches, dining, PBI airport, and the Brightline are all minutes away.
